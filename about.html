CropView Drone

Agritech drones are revolutionizing crop monitoring by providing farmers with a high-resolution, real-time view of their fields. Equipped with advanced sensors like multispectral, hyperspectral, and thermal cameras, drones can detect subtle changes in crop health, identify issues like pests or diseases, and optimize resource management. This technology helps farmers reduce waste, increase yields, and minimize environmental impact.

Weather Track Station

Weather tracking station in Agritech refers to a specialized weather station designed to monitor and collect weather data crucial for agricultural operations. These stations, often incorporating multiple sensors, provide real-time information to farmers on various weather parameters like temperature, humidity, wind speed, solar radiation, and rainfall. This data is then used to optimize farming practices, improve crop management, and make informed decisions regarding irrigation, pest control, and harvesting.
